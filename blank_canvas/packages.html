Packages

We recognize that circumstances vary and desire all creators to be able to share their vision and talent with the world.

Image by Freepik

Daisy Package

At $10 per month or $100 per year, you gain the ability to design your portfolio using our predesigned layouts, access to our member's forum where artists can find support along with helpful advice on social media management, portfolio design, and how to price your artwork. You will also recieve tech support, our basic sales software, and our social media management system.

Image by Freepik

Rose Package

At $20 per month of $200 per year, you gain access to the ability to change the basic layouts to better suit your needs, our commission based sales software, and access to a zoom masterclass on different aspects of building a web presence once a month. We will also spend a mazimum of one hour helping you with design your logo and connecting to the different social media platforms.

Photo by Kier in Sight on Unsplash

Laurel Package

At $40 per month or $400 per year, you recieve everything offered above along with an individual design for your portfolio, 24/7 live tech support, and the opportunity to be featured as artist of the month in our membership forum. This also includes logistic support when it comes to shipping your artwork in the United States.
