Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to convince customers to use Blank Canvas to design and host their online portfolios, and to share our product with others in their circles. The end goal is to gain Blank Canvas customers and revenue.

Audience

Our audience are mainly newly or unestablished artists who are reaching out to their customers and followers through social media. They will have a wide variety of disposable income and will want different packages that balance maximum usability and features with minimum cost. They need a place to consolidate their brand and to facilitate the purchase of their art. Once we meet their needs, they will become a loyal customer base. Most will be using mobile device for access to the website, but will need laptop and tablet compatibility as well.

Content

Home page

What we do? We seek to create a world in which art of all forms is recognized and celebrated. Dedicated to helping artists share their passion and reach new audiences, we seek to create a community of creators who support eachother and celebrate the diversity, beauty, and meaning found in the world around us. Our online portfolios not only help you to define and solidify your brand, but facilitates your use of social media and the ability to sell to your customers. You will find that we offer support during every step of your journey and encourage you to share your talents, experience, and knowledge with the other artists around you.

Why us? Our foremost priority is helping you to share your art with the world. We put your creativity, individuality, and self expression first. We understand your drive and passion, and simply hands you the tools you need to share that with the world. At all times, you keep complete control of your art and how it is used and shared. You have control over sales and commissions. You remain charge of your artistic identity. We do not try to fit you in a box of what the world thinks art and artists should be, but free you to express yourself in a thousand different ways.

Packages

We recognize that circumstances vary and desire for all creators to be able to share their talent with the world. For this reason we offer 3 different packages for our customers.

The first is the Daisy Package. At $10 per month or $100 per year, you gain the ability to design your portfolio using our predesigned layouts, access to our member's forum where artists can find support along with helpful advice on social media management, portfolio design, and how to price your artwork. You will also recieve tech support, our basic sales software, and our social media management system.

The second is the Rose Package. At $20 per month of $200 per year, you gain access to the ability to change the basic layouts to better suit your needs, our commission based sales software, and access to a zoom masterclass on different aspects of building a web presence once a month. We will also spend a mazimum of one hour helping you with design your logo and connecting to the different social media platforms.

The final is the Laurel Package. At $40 per month or $400 per year, you recieve everything offered above along with an individual design for your portfolio, 24/7 live tech support, and the opportunity to be featured as artist of the month in our membership forum. This also includes logistic support when it comes to shipping your artwork in the United States.

Resources

Membership forum Here you have access to support from other artists along with the ability to share your own experiences, helpful advice on the building an online presence, and how pricing works. We will also feature one artist or artform every month to encourage the celebration of diversity and sharing of ideas.

Logo Builder This is a tool to help you design a presonal logo or crafter's mark. Having an individual and recognizable signature plays a vital part in building brand recognition, especially for artists who enjoy exploring a variety of mediums.

Portfolio design tool This is the tool those who purshase the Daisy and Rose Package will use to design their portfolios. It includes preset layouts, a colour pallete tool, and the ability to add your own images as background/elements of your portfolio.

Tech support This is how members can reach out to us if problems arise with their portfolio. We promise to contact and resolve errors within 48 hours or offer a complete discount for that month of membership.
