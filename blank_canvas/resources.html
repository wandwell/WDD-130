Resources

Logo Builder

This is a tool to help you design a presonal logo or crafter's mark. Having an individual and recognizable signature plays a vital part in building brand recognition, especially for artists who enjoy exploring a variety of mediums.

Portfolio Designer

This is the tool those who purshase the Daisy and Rose Package will use to design their portfolios. It includes preset layouts, a colour pallete tool, and the ability to add your own images as background/elements of your portfolio. This is where you choose how you want your art displayed.

Membership Forum

Here you have access to support from other artists along with the ability to share your own experiences, helpful advice on the building an online presence, and how pricing works. We will also feature one artist or artform every month to encourage the celebration of diversity and sharing of ideas.

Tech & Logistic Support

This is how members can reach out to us if problems arise with their portfolio. We promise to contact and resolve errors within 48 hours or offer a complete discount for that month of membership.
